Gekkoukan High School   
The school Yukari, Mitsuru, and the others attend. It stands at the center of a man-made island known as Tatsumi Port Island.   
   
Gekkoukan High School   
The school that the members of the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) attend. By day, it is an ordinary place of learning; but by night, it turns into Tartarus.   
   
Gekkoukan High School   
The school that the members of the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) attend. It used to be the testing ground for Shadow research and was involved in a major incident ten years ago.   
   
Iwatodai Dorm   
A place where students attending Gekkoukan High live. Normally, boys and girls would be housed in separate buildings, but due to certain circumstances, it is a co-ed dormitory.   
   
Iwatodai Dorm   
The dorm where members of the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) who attend Gekkoukan High live. It is also their base of operations.   
   
Contract   
The contract made with the mysterious boy at the entrance of the dorm. It says, "I choose this fate of mine own free will."   
   
Contract   
The contract made with Pharos at the entrance of the dorm. It says, "I choose this fate of mine own free will."   
   
Velvet Room   
An ultramarine blue room that exists between dream and reality, mind and matter. A place only those who have fulfilled their "contract" are allowed to visit.   
   
Velvet Key   
A key that unlocks the door to the Velvet Room. It has a brilliant ultramarine hue.   
   
Apathy Syndrome   
A mysterious syndrome that has been spreading recently. A healthy person will suddenly become lethargic and unresponsive the next day. The news claims it to be stress-related...   
   
Apathy Syndrome   
A mysterious syndrome taking over the streets. In reality, it is what becomes of those who were attacked by Shadows and turned into "the Lost."   
   
Dark Hour   
An anomalous time that occurs every day at midnight. Those without the potential transmogrify and cannot perceive it.   
   
Dark Hour   
An anomalous time that occurs every day at midnight. All machinery shuts down. Backup equipment must be custom-made to function during the Dark Hour.   
   
Dark Hour   
It began after an experiment went wrong, ten years ago. Twelve Shadows were dispersed as a result of the accident, and it is said that the Dark Hour will disappear if those Shadows are defeated.   
   
Dark Hour   
A time outside of time. The manifestation of Shadows' true power. This phenomenon serves as an omen of "the Fall."   
   
Transmogrification   
A phenomenon where regular people without the potential transform into coffin-like objects during the Dark Hour. Those who undergo this transformation retain no memory of it.   
   
Potential   
A predisposition in a person that allows Persona-users to function normally during the Dark Hour without being transmogrified.   
   
Persona   
An entity that appears when a user unleashes their potential. According to Igor, it is a manifestation of one's inner power and personality. Its appearance varies depending on the user.   
   
Social Link   
The bond that forms through interaction with other people. Not only is it essential to sustain human community, it is also the key that determines the strength of a user's Persona abilities.   
   
Shadow   
Mysterious creatures. They only appear during the Dark Hour and attack any who weren't transmogrified. Their victims develop peculiar symptoms and are referred to as "the Lost."   
   
Shadow   
A creature that feeds on the psyches of those who roam free during the Dark Hour. Their purpose is unknown. They can be sorted into twelve different categories.   
   
SEES   
A group that was established to fight against Shadows. On the outside, it looks like a regular school club. The letters "S.E.E.S." on the armband stand for **S**pecialized **E**xtracurricular **E**xecution **S**quad.   
   
Evoker   
An auxiliary device, usually shaped like a gun, that is used to summon Personas. Each Persona-user has their own. However, in theory, a Persona can be summoned even without the use of one.   
   
Tartarus   
A mysterious labyrinth in which the Shadows nest. It only appears during the Dark Hour. It is said to hold the key to unraveling the secret behind the Dark Hour.   
   
Kirijo Group   
One of the world's leading multinational companies, they boast a large market share in various industries. They also funded the establishment of Gekkoukan High.   
   
Full Moon   
When the moon waxes full. A night on which hardships will arise... as told by the mysterious boy.   
   
Full Moon   
A night on which hardship arises, as foretold by Pharos. Large Shadows appear in the city during the full moon.   
   
The Voice   
The voice of the Shadows. Those who hear it won't undergo transmogrification, but since they don't have the potential, this is a dangerous state to be in.   
   
Theurgy   
An immensely powerful attack that unleashes when the user's emotions synchronize under the right conditions.   
   
Combat Gear   
The official combat uniform of SEES, custom-made for each member's unique combat style. The armbands also come equipped with a variety of features.   
   
The Incident Ten Years Ago   
An accident that occurred when an experiment by the Kirijo Group went wrong. It caused the Dark Hour and Tartarus to emerge. However, this truth was never disclosed to the general public.   
   
The Kirijo Group and the Nanjo Group   
The Kirijo Group was formed after separating from the Nanjo Group. The family motto, "Two in harmony surpasses one in perfection," was passed down to the Kirijo Group back then, as well.   
   
Time Manipulation Device   
A device that the previous head of the Kirijo Group, Kouetsu, attempted to create by using the power of the Shadows. It was supposed to control the flow of time, and therefore influence the future.   
   
Aigis   
The final anti-Shadow weapon created ten years ago, and the only one remaining. It possesses the appearance and mental fortitude of a human, and can also summon a Persona.   
   
Anti-Shadow Weapon   
Combat weapons that were developed ten years ago during the experiments, as insurance in the event a Shadow became unmanageable.   
   
Strega   
A hostile, mysterious duo who appear to also be Persona-users. They draw the attention of the public with their bizarre appearance and unusual presence.   
   
Strega   
A hostile, mysterious trio of Persona-users. Only the girl, Chidori, has been taken into custody, but the group's true identity is still unknown.   
   
Strega   
A hostile, mysterious trio of Persona-users. They possess a wildly different perspective of life and death than average civilians do. After the momentous battle with SEES, Takaya and Jin threw themselves into the sea.   
   
Strega   
A trio of hostile Persona-users. To save Junpei from Takaya and Jin, who had stealthily survived their jump, Chidori sacrificed her life and her soul departed.   
   
Strega   
A trio of hostile Persona-users. Chidori was miraculously revived; however, Takaya and Jin will likely make their appearance at Tartarus on the Promised Day.   
   
Strega   
Hostile Persona-users. They preach about the coming of Nyx by manipulating the media. Their leader is known as the "Messiah" on the streets. They will likely be at Tartarus on the Promised Day.   
   
Suppressant   
The drug that Aragaki used to maintain control over his body and Persona. Originally obtained from Strega; it's said to have side effects that can even be life-threatening.   
   
Accident Two Years Ago   
When taking down a large Shadow, Aragaki lost control of his Persona. A victim got caught up in it, and lost their life. That victim happened to be Amada's mother.   
   
Death   
An "Almighty Existence" that Ikutsuki was plotting to revive. Its birth was already intended to happen once, ten years ago. It is said to have the power to bring forth "the Fall."   
   
Death   
Ryoji's true identity. A superior entity that would have been born ten years ago, had a certain experiment not been interrupted. Bearer of the 13th Arcana and the Appriser of Death.   
   
Nyx   
The mother of Shadows who, in ancient times, bestowed Death upon this world. If she awakens, every being will transmogrify into the Lost and the entire planet will fall to darkness.   
   
New Year's Eve   
The day that a decision has to be made about Ryoji. Whether to kill him and forget everything, or stand and fight against the inevitable... The fateful decision awaits.   
   
End of the World   
January 31st... The day that Nyx descends upon Tartarus. Nyx can be faced on this day by ascending to the summit of Tartarus.   
   
Graduation   
Graduation ceremony for the 2009 school year. You promised to meet up with your friends on graduation day, even if everyone loses their memories.
