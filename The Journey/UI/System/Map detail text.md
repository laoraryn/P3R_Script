Gekkoukan School   
A private institute funded by the Kirijo Group that includes elementary, middle, and high school. Established in 1982, with the building rebuilt since.   
   
Iwatodai Dorm   
Formerly a hotel, renovated as a dormitory for Gekkoukan High students. Unlike other school dorms, it is inhabited by both boys and girls.   
   
Paulownia Mall   
A large mall located in the vast shopping area in the northern part of Port Island.   
   
Iwatodai Station   
The last stop of the passenger train Anehazuru. One of the two entryways to Port Island.   
   
Iwatodai Strip Mall   
A strip mall built at Iwatodai Station, the connecting station of the local train line and the monorail.   
   
Naganaki Shrine   
A historic shrine located in Iwatodai.
