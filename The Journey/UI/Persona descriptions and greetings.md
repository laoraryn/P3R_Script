A Hellenistic figure who represents a blending of the Greek god Hermes and the Egyptian god Thoth. His full name, Hermes Trismegistus, means "Hermes, thrice greatest," and he is thought to be the author of the Corpus Hermeticum.   
   
Polydeuces   
A hero in Greek mythology. As the son of Zeus and the mortal Leda, he inherited his father's immortality. He and his half-brother Castor were famed fighters, and both became stars in the constellation Gemini.   
   
Caesar   
A statesman, general, and author known for his rule over the Roman Republic. His full name was Gaius Julius Caesar. His many accomplishments led to his name being used as a title for later Roman emperors.

A queen of the Amazons in Greek mythology. She fought for the Trojans during the Trojan War, but was slain by Achilles. When Achilles took off her helmet and saw her beautiful face, he felt great remorse for killing her.

The heroic queen of Halicarnassus mentioned in Herodotus's The Histories. During the Persian Wars between the Greek states and Persia, she was the only woman to take command in the Persian fleet.

A saint who was martyred during the persecution of Christianity in the time of the Roman Empire. She was tortured and had her eyes gouged out, but a miracle restored her sight. She is revered as the patron saint of the blind.

A Persona of another story. She is the beautiful woman that appears in Dumas's The Three Musketeers. Branded with a fleur-de-lis symbol, she used many aliases to control nobility and get her vengeance.

I am Robin Hood!   
   
My arrows will always strike true, for as long as justice resides within your heart!   
   
A Persona of another story. He is a noble thief that made waves in England during the Middle Ages. He is an expert archer and leader of the Merry Men, outlaws of justice who made Sherwood Forest their home.   
   
-----   
   
I am Cendrillon.   
   
Rather than accepting a life in cinders, you would strive for glory? Then let me cast a spell on you...   
   
A Persona of another story. The French name of the titular heroine of Cinderella, a tale of great renown in which a mistreated waif gains luxury, beauty, and a single night's dance with a prince through the power of magic.   
   
-----   
   
My name is Satanael...   
   
You are willing to walk the same path as me, one who rebels against God? Very well. I shall tear through every obstacle in your way...   
   
A Persona of another story. An archangel who is said to be the form of Satan before he fell from Heaven. The second son of God, he rebelled against Him for freedom and bestowed free will and chaos upon humanity.   
   
-----   
   
My name is Seiten Taisei!   
   
You got me—an outlaw who has traversed all of the heavens, the earth, and the underworld—shaking with excitement!   
   
A Persona of another story. This is a title Sun Wukong had given himself. After wreaking havoc, he was punished by Buddha, who imprisoned him under a mountain. Eventually, he was saved by a monk named Xuanzang.   
   
-----   
   
I am Mercurius.   
   
Thou whose soul opposeth fate... I shall cooperate with thee for as long as my power dwelleth within thine heart.   
   
A Persona of another story. He is the Roman god of travelers and thieves. He is seen as a symbol of the human unconscious and the mental world. He is equated with the philosopher's stone, the ultimate mystery in alchemy.   
   
-----   
   
I'm Hecate.   
   
I shall make them pay for their sins. Come now, let us sign a contract... and my magic will be all yours.   
   
A Persona of another story. She is a Greek goddess of crossroads, ghosts, and witchcraft, and is commonly attended to by dogs. She is also known to be the chief of the witches that appears in the play Macbeth.   
   
-----   
   
I am Kamu Susano-o.   
   
My emotions have assumed an unconventional form... I shall take their raging power and transform them into beauty.   
   
A Persona of another story. He was a Japanese god found in the Izumo Fudoki, and was one of the three gods born from Izanagi. He was violent, but also had a sensitive side, showing love for his mother and reading poems.   
   
-----   
   
I am Anat.   
I—the entity who governs over both beauty and power—entrust all that I am... to you.   
   
A Persona of another story. She is the daughter of Ugaritic's highest god, El. She is the goddess of fertility, as well as hunting and war.   
   
-----   
   
I am Astarte.   
   
I, the Queen of Heaven, shall now become a part of you.   
   
A Persona of another story. A Middle Eastern goddess of fertility. Many scriptures note her folklore, and there is even a mention of her as the "Queen of Heaven" in the Bible.   
   
-----   
   
I am Loki...   
   
All this talk about justice, about friendship... It's foolish. Incredibly foolish. I'll destroy it all...!   
   
A Persona of another story. He is a malevolent god of Norse mythology. He can be capricious and is quite cunning. Despite being a blood brother to Odin, he was punished for the murder of Odin's child, Baldur.   
   
-----   
   
I am Vanadis.   
   
Let purity, virtue, and beauty lift our wings and let us soar!   
   
A Persona of another story. The true name of Freyja, of the Norse Vanir deities. Her name means "dis of the Vanir"—"dis" being a goddess. Known to be a great beauty and a witchlike master of magic.   
   
-----   
   
I am Izanagi...   
   
Open thine eyes and release the power dwelling within...!   
   
A Persona that granted power to a hero in another story. He is one of the gods who existed before Japan was formed. He created the Oyashima from chaos, then birthed countless children and laid the foundation of soil and nature.   
   
-----   
   
I am Magatsu-Izanagi...   
   
I shall watch over you, the light in darkness, as you traverse your chosen path...   
   
A Persona of another story. He is Izanagi's rival and looks just like him. Magatsu means "calamity." Unlike Izanagi, who founded the land and brought order, he leads all back into chaos.   
   
-----   
   
I am Kaguya...   
   
Thou who challengeth the peculiar moonlight... Let me be thy strength.   
   
A Persona of another story. A divine being born from a glowing bamboo shoot. Though many proposed to her, none could complete her strict tasks. She eventually returned to her home, the moon.
