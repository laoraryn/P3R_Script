Gekkoukan High School   
The school that the members of the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) attend. It used to be the testing ground for Shadow research and was involved in a major incident ten years ago.   
   
---   
   
Iwatodai Dorm   
The Gekkoukan High dorm where members of the Specialized Extracurricular Execution Squad (SEES) lived. It is scheduled for closure on April 1st.   
   
---   
   
Velvet Room   
An ultramarine blue room that exists between dream and reality, mind and matter. A place only those who have fulfilled their "contract" are allowed to visit.   
   
---   
   
Velvet Key   
A key that unlocks the door to the Velvet Room. It has a brilliant ultramarine hue.   
   
---   
   
Apathy Syndrome   
A mysterious syndrome taking over the streets. In reality, it is what becomes of those who were attacked by Shadows and turned into "the Lost."   
   
---   
   
Dark Hour   
A phenomenon that took place every midnight, during which humans transmogrified into coffins and manmade devices stopped functioning. Following the operations of SEES, this phenomenon has stopped occurring.   
   
---   
   
Personas   
Entities that appear when users unleash their potential. According to Igor, they are manifestations of their users' inner power and personalities. Their appearances vary depending on the user.   
   
---   
   
Shadow   
A creature that fed on the psyches of those who roamed free during the Dark Hour. Their purpose is unknown. They can be sorted into twelve different categories.   
   
---   
   
Shadow   
A creature that fed on the psyches of those who roamed free during the Dark Hour. They were believed to have vanished along with Tartarus, but have since appeared in the Abyss of Time, attacking Aigis and her allies.   
   
---   
   
SEES   
A group that was established to fight against Shadows. On the outside, it looks like a regular school club. The letters "S.E.E.S." on the armband stand for **S**pecialized **E**xtracurricular **E**xecution **S**quad.   
   
---   
   
Evoker   
An auxiliary device, usually shaped like a gun, that is used to summon Personas. Each Persona-user has their own. However, in theory, a Persona can be summoned even without the use of one.   
   
---   
   
Tartarus   
An immense tower that Gekkoukan High School would transform into due to the effects of Shadows. Following the operations of SEES, these transformations no longer occur.   
   
---   
   
Theurgy   
An immensely powerful attack that unleashes when the user's emotions synchronize under the right conditions.   
   
---   
   
Combat Gear   
The official combat uniform of SEES, custom-made for each member's unique combat style. The armbands also come equipped with a variety of features.   
   
---   
   
The Incident Ten Years Ago   
An accident that occurred when an experiment by the Kirijo Group went wrong. It caused the Dark Hour and Tartarus to emerge. However, this truth was never disclosed to the general public.   
   
---   
   
Aigis   
The final anti-Shadow weapon created ten years ago, and the only one remaining. It possesses the appearance and mental fortitude of a human, and can also summon a Persona.   
   
---   
   
Strega   
A group of Persona-users that stood in opposition to SEES. They propagated the ideology of the end of the world as salvation, and were believed by many to be prophetic.   
   
---   
   
Accident Two Years Ago   
When taking down an irregular Shadow, Aragaki lost control of his Persona. A victim got caught up in it, and lost their life. That victim happened to be Amada's mother.   
   
---   
   
Nyx   
The mother of Shadows who, in ancient times, bestowed Death upon this world. SEES chose to face her in order to prevent her awakening from eradicating all life.   
   
---   
   
That Day   
The day everyone promised to meet up, even if they lost their memories. It would be the last day Aigis ever spoke to him.   
   
---   
   
Shadow   
Mysterious monsters that appeared in Tartarus and now the Abyss of Time. Like Personas, they are actually humans' suppressed thoughts given physical form.   
   
---   
   
Wild Card   
The ability to hold multiple Personas at once and switch between their powers at will. He had this skill as well.   
   
---   
   
Time Loop   
The phenomenon wherein space and time has become distorted by the effects of the Abyss of Time, causing the same day to repeat endlessly.   
   
---   
   
Metis   
A mysterious figure and sister unit of Aigis. She claims to have come to protect her "sister." However, Aigis is known to be the last unit of her kind.   
   
---   
   
Metis   
She awoke in the Colosseo Purgatorio knowing nothing but that she has a sister who could understand her, and that this sister would die if she didn't do something.   
   
---   
   
Abyss of Time   
Uncharted territory that spreads out like tree roots beneath Iwatodai Dorm. Though once a small hollow, it has grown significantly, becoming inextricably connected.   
   
---   
   
Abyss of Time   
A void that formed in reaction to Tartarus's creation. It should have disappeared when Tartarus did, but has been kept in place by a force seemingly connected to Aigis and her allies.   
   
---   
   
Sanada's Past   
While both in middle school, Mitsuru tracked down Sanada to enlist him in her fight against the Shadows. Intrigued by the chance to battle a new kind of opponent, he decided to join Mitsuru in her crusade against the Dark Hour.   
   
---   
   
Junpei's Past   
To avoid his alcoholic father, Junpei would frequently make it a point to not be at home. One day, an aimless Junpei told a passing white dog, Koromaru, about his longing for some kind of special skill.   
   
---   
   
Mitsuru's Past   
Accompanying her father and his retinue on an investigation of Tartarus, a young Mitsuru summoned a Persona for the first time to save her father from a Shadow attack. Her fate has been tied to fighting Shadows ever since.   
   
---   
   
Yukari's Past   
Yukari had been considering giving up her search for the truth behind her father's death. However, her resolve was reignited after receiving a letter written by her father ten years ago.   
   
---   
   
Aigis's Past   
For a time, Aigis had a recurring dream in which she would wake in a formless void to see him, then run after him as he walked away. One day, she stopped having the dream and was freed from her anguish.   
   
---   
   
Keys of Time   
Keys obtained by Aigis and her allies following a significant victory in the Abyss of Time. All eight are needed to open either the dorm entrance door to the present or his room's door to the past.   
   
---   
   
True Key   
All eight keys fused together to form this one. Aigis obtained it after prevailing in combat against her allies.
